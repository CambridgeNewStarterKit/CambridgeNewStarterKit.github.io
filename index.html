University of Cambridge New Starter Guide

Welcome to Cambridge!

The purpose of this website is to be a point of first call for new arrivals to this beautiful city. Here, you will find top tips on necessities that every Cambridge student needs and where to buy them. Additionally, you can use our ‘for sale and exchange’ section to browse for second hand items offered by locals or to advertise items that you would like to sell yourself. This can include things like bikes, gowns or formal tickets. Finally, we also provide links to other fantastic websites, which are an excellent resource for new starters.

Happy browsing and we hope you find what you are looking for!
